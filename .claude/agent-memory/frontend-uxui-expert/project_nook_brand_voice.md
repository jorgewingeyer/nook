Nook is an Argentine boutique home decor brand. Philosophy: "No vendemos objetos. Creamos rincones."

**Tone:** Warm, human, sophisticated but accessible. Boutique luxury feel — not cold marketplace, not generic e-commerce.

**Key copy rules:**
- Use "vos" (never "usted" or "tú")
- Argentine imperative: "Explorá", "Agregá", "Encontrá", "Seguí" — never "Añadir", "Buscar" (as imperatives)
- "Piezas" not "productos" in editorial/emotional contexts; "producto" is OK in functional/transactional UI
- "Rincones", "refugios", "hogar" — brand vocabulary
- Empty states are an invitation, not just an error
- Confirmation moments (order success) are celebrations, not just data

**Established copy patterns (confirmed in codebase):**
- Cart empty: "Tu carrito está esperando algo especial"
- Order success h1: "¡Qué buena elección!"
- Order success body: "Tu pedido está en camino a convertirse en parte de tu hogar. Te escribimos cuando despache."
- Out of stock (button/detail): "Temporalmente sin stock"
- Out of stock (tiny card badge): "Sin stock" (kept short for space constraints)
- Free shipping bar: "Sumá X más y el envío te sale gratis"
- Back to browse: "Seguir explorando" / "Explorar la colección"
- Featured badge: "Favorito" (was "Destacado")
- Hero stats: "Hogares transformados" (was "Clientes")
- Checkout h1: "Casi listo — completá tu pedido"

**Why:** Jorge confirmed this direction; tone aligns with brand philosophy and Argentine market.
**How to apply:** Any time copy is written or reviewed for Nook UI — check tone matches boutique warmth, not cold e-commerce defaults.
